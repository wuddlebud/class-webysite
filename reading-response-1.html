Quotes that stood out/ clarifying words-

“For Victor Ocampo” (who is that)? (irrelevant question)

“Inexorable death” Inexorable means a person or force that cannot be stopped

“ Besides, I know of a man from England --a modest man--who for me is no less great than Goethe.” Johan Wolfgang von Goethe was a famous German polymath and writer. He’s regarded as the most influential writer in the German language. ( a polymath is a person with a lot of knowledge about different, often unrelated things)

“abject felicity” Abject means a state of extreme sadness or misery, the lowest a person could feel. Felicity almost means the opposite, and intense happiness or bliss.

“Inviolate” means a sacred and untouched place

“The instructions to turn always to the left reminded me that such was the common procedure for discovering the central point of certain Labyrinth” (page 4) weird relation, but this sort of reminds me of how when navigating in our computer using the terminal, it seems we’ve been restricted in our movements to commands that allow us to move through certain paths (back through the parent folder, it seems you have to follow the path)
